About Me

Career Changer, Rock'N Roller, Dog Lover, Occasional Athlete

I developed a passion for coding at a young age. Though I never coded professionally, I always gravitated towards software, tools, and services designed to make my job easier. After spending 10 years working in Investment Banking and earning my MBA part time, I decided to pivot into technology, by completing coursework in programming and website design.

As a Product Manager and Project Manager in the fintech space, I spent considerable time working with the software development lifecycle, leading enterprise software implementations, collaborating closely with programmers, and managing client relationships. Though I was not writing code, I truly enjoyed solving business problems through software.

It took a global pandemic to make me realize that life is too short to spend it doing things you do not have a passion for. I have decided to pursue a career as a Software Engineer mainly because:

  • Software Engineering combines creativity and problem-solving, allowing individuals to express themselves and find innovative solutions.

  • The continuous learning aspect of coding keeps developers engaged and excited as they explore new technologies and stay up to date.

  • Software Engineering provides the ability to build something meaningful, collaborate with a supportive community, flexibility, and autonomy.

I am truly excited for the next chapter in my career!
